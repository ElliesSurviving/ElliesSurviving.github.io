About This Site!

Mac OS 7. Packing all the latest and greatest HTML4 features, this is essentally the best way to

Like many others we wanted to have our own corner on the World Wide Web, and help work towards
a web that doesnt become shit, and instead becomes fun to surf, where each site is unique, and becomes
a place where friendships are made!
